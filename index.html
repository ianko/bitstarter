Are you a service provider and need to find concentrations of people who are likely to need your services? So, the Where's my next customer? will help you to mine the social networks and show the potential customers for your business.

You will need to configure some keywords that characterize your business and, optionally, some characteristhics of your prospective customer. Whenever the criteria is satisfied, you will receive the information of your next potential customer and few options to approach and get introduced to them by social proximity with your contacts.
